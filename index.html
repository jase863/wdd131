Germany

The official flag of Germany

Germany is located in Western Europe between France and Austria. It has a land mass of 138,000 sqaure miles, which is roughly the size of the US state of Montana. Unlike Montana, however, Germany has a fairly dense population for its size. The estimated population of Germany is 84,075,075 people, which is almost 83 times that of Montana. The official language of Germany is German. Three of its neighbors - Austria, Liechtenstein, and Switzerland - also share Germany as their official language.
